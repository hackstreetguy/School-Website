Hostel facility

The Lav Kush Senior Secondary School, being exclusively residential, provides seperate hostels for boys and girls. For the present, we provided dormitory accommodation for students. However, with the addition of higher classes, individual rooms will be made available.

School Hostal Building

The hostel maintains high standards of hygiene and cleanliness.

To ensure that students live in a secure and safe environment, the hostels have round-the-clock security arrangements.

The school has a large dining hall with a modern and well-equipped kitchen. Professionally trained staff ensure that the food provided is nutritious and well balanced. Strict quality control is enforced through daily inspection by assigned staff.
